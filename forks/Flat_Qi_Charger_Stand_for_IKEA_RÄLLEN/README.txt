# Summary

UPDATE: Want a better [Qi Charger Stand for IKEA RÄLLEN](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2678724/)? 

IKEA's RÄLLEN Qi charger doesn't sit well on it's own. I made this friction fit base to accommodate RÄLLEN, with a small cord channel so that the assembly can sit flat. 

Printed at 30% infill on a Monoprice Maker Select Plus v2, with Woodfill filament.

Also: rallen, since apparently the accents make rallen unsearchable. RALLEN
