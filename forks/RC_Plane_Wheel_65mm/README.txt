# Summary

I've designed these wheels for my Hall Bulldog Racer plane.
They are 65mm diameter and 20mm wide, fit perfectly in the wheel pants I designed for them (check that other design).
I printed the hub in ABS and the tyre in TPU, but I think PLA for the hub will work just fine.
